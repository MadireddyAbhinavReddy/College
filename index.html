About MVSR Engineering College

Established in 1981 and affiliated to Osmania University, MVSR Engineering College offers a diverse range of UG and PG programs including B.E., M.E., and MBA. The college stands out as a research center recognized for Ph.D. programs in CSE, ECE, and Mechanical Engineering. With a yearly intake of around 1000 students, MVSR has consistently ranked among the top private engineering colleges in Telangana, known for its academic excellence and industry relevance.

PVR Kashyap

Chairman

Mr. P.V.R. Kashyap, a dynamic entrepreneur and mentor, leads MVSR with a vision rooted in innovation and social responsibility. He co-founded the PV Narasimha Rao Foundation and actively supports education, healthcare, and environmental causes across India.

Dr. M. Kameswara Rao

Principal

Dr. M. Kameswara Rao, Professor at MVSR Engineering College since 2017, has 30 years of teaching experience and a Ph.D. from IIT Bombay. He has published 50+ papers, guided research, reviewed key texts, and is a Fellow of IEI, IAStructE, and a life member of ISTE.
